    • E-COMMERCE AND ITS IMPACT ON BUSINESS


    Technology is evolving at a very fast rate in this 21st century, and individuals from different parts of the globe are making numerous suppliments on it. The influence of technology on our daily lives,business can not be denied.Humans use technology to travel, to communicate, to learn, to do business and to live in comfort.In this era of technology, individuals,or company are not only trying to grow their company in one portion or nation, so they are using distinct kinds of technology which help to access the entire world.So technology provide us ecommerce,which helps in growing business, sharing good and services anu many more.In short, E-commerce means the purchase and sales of products,good and services using electronic means such as mobile apps ,different websites and the Internet.Money, funds and information transactions are also regarded to be e-commerce. Over the past decades, ecommerce has grown enormously in popularity, replacing traditional brick and mortar shops in ways.Ecommerce allows us to purchase and sell goods globally 24 hours a day.While most individuals consider ecommerce to be a consumer to company there are many other kinds of ecommerce such as online auction sites, online banking, online ticketing and reservations, and transactions from company to company .E-commerce between companies varies from that between a company and its customers.There are five generally accepted types of e-commerce: - Business to Business (B2B) - Business to Consumer (B2C) - Consumer to Business (C2B) - Consumer to Consumer (C2C) - Business to Government (B2G).

    ADVANTAGES and DISADVANTAGES OF E-COMMERCE


    There's an old adage that I'm sure is going to sound familiar. It suggests there are two sides to a coin. We don't try to talk about the morally good or bad, but we want to talk about the advantages and the desadvantages that an e-commerce company provides.
    Advantages of E-commerce.
    1.Organizations can use e-commerce to expand their market with minimum capital investment to domestic and foreign markets. An organisation across the globe can readily find more clients, best vendors, and appropriate company associates.
    2.No geographical limitations translates as a bigger customer reach
    3.It offers data that is easily accessible. In seconds, a customer can see the relevant detailed information and not wait for days or weeks
    4.Much faster transactions available 24/7
    5.E-commerce enables government deliver public services such as health care, education, social services at a lower price and in an enhanced way.
    Disadvantage Of E-Commerce
    1.Because there is minimal direct customer-company interaction, customer loyalty becomes a major problem.
    2.Anyone can begin an online business, leading to scams and phishing sites sometimes.
    3.Online transactions are always faced with the risk of insecurity. There is no Cash On Delivery (COD) alternative for many locations. Giving all the card information while making internet payment is dangerous for clients.
    4.No guarantee of product quality.
    5.Even though e-commerce provides much more data than traditional retail about a product or service, it does not allow you to experience any of it before you buy.
